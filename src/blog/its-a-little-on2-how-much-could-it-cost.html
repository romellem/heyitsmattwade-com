You’re slinging some code, vibing as Copilot blissfully autocompletes the bits and bobs. You start writing your next chunk:

{% highlight js %} const itemsById = {% endhighlight %}

And you don’t give a second thought as you tab into this beauty

{% highlight js %} const itemsById = items.reduce( (acc, items) => ({ ...acc, [item.id]: item, }), {}, ); {% endhighlight %}

It’s terse, immutable, and most importantly, it’s pretty one-liner ✨💅. You push straight to main and call it a day.

What you might not have realized is that you just took what should have been an O(n) operation and made it O(n²). You just had to have your terseness, your immutability, your fp snobbery.
